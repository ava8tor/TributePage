Picture of Kazuki Takahashi

Brief Timeline of Takahashi's life

  • 1961 - Born in Tokyo, Japan
  • 1981 - Won the Shogakukan New Comic Award for his one-shot manga, Ing! Love Ball
  • 1986 - Debut his first serial, Gō-Q-Chōji Ikkiman in Kodansha's Weekly Shōnen Magazine.
  • 1990 - Published a one-shot manga, Tokiō no Taka, in Weekly Shōnen Jump
  • 1994 - Publish the manga series Yu-Gi-Oh! in Weekly Shōnen Jump. Was serialized until 2004 and sold over 40 million copies.
  • 1996 - The first animated adaption for Yu-Gi-Oh was released
  • 1999 - A trading card Game based on Yu-Gi-Oh was produced and released by Konami
  • 2011 - More than 25.1 billion cards of the Trading Card game were sold (holding the Guinness World Record for best-selling trading card game)
  • 2015 - Received the Inkpot Award from Comic-Con International for his outstanding contributions to comics
  • 2018 - Published a limited manga series, the Comiq, in Weekly Shōnen Jump
  • 2022 - Passed away a the age of 60 from reported assistance of a search and rescue of 3 swimmers caught in a rip current.
